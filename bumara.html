What We Do

Bumara is organized into four connected modules designed to work together.

Compliance Hub

A unified dashboard to monitor, manage, and complete obligations across key regulators. Track by status, get deadline reminders, and maintain a complete audit trail.

Smart Invoicing

Professional, branded invoicing with built-in VAT calculations, customer management, and invoice history. Compliance-ready records from day one.

Automated Payroll

A fully automated gross-to-net payroll engine that calculates PAYE, social security, and health insurance. Payroll processed in minutes, not days.

Document Management

Centralized storage with structured folders, version history, role-based access control, and expiry tracking for licenses, certificates, and contracts.
