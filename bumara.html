About Bumara

The Operating System
for Growth

One platform, endless possibilities.

Bumara is a compliance-first business operations platform built for real world business realities. We're moving businesses from the era of "paper and portals" to integrated operations.

The Challenge

In the old world, businesses operated in silos - struggling with multiple portals, scattered files, and missed deadlines that lead to heavy penalties.

In the new world, compliance regulators are going digital, demanding real-time accuracy and instant transparency.

The Losers

Businesses stuck in "manual chaos," struggling with multiple portals, scattered files, and missed deadlines.

The Winners

Businesses that treat compliance as a built-in advantage, scaling faster with confidence.

What We Do

We bring regulatory obligations into one connected system.

Smart Invoicing

Professional, branded invoicing with built-in VAT calculations and customer management. Accurate fiscal record-keeping that ensures you're audit-ready from day one.

Automated Payroll

A fully automated gross-to-net payroll engine that calculates PAYE, social security, and health insurance. Payroll processed in minutes - not days.

Bumara Pay

A dedicated payments layer to pay regulatory fees, payroll, and business expenses. Every transaction automatically linked to the relevant obligation.
